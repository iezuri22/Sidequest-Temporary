How It Works

Three steps to shipping faster

01

Build Your Profile

Add your work entries — your startup, side project, freelance gig, 9-to-5. AI breaks your goals into actionable subtasks so you always know what's next.

02

Join or Host Events

Find in-person coworking sessions near you, or host your own. Set the vibe — free or paid, open or approval-only, any size from 5 to 50.

03

Session Showcase

At the end of every session, present what you worked on. It's the signature Sidequest moment — accountability meets celebration.

Features

Everything you need to ship

🤖

AI-Powered Subtasks

Describe your project goal and AI breaks it into 15–60 minute subtasks. Re-split any task that feels too big. Get in-task help via chat.

👥

Real Collaborators

Invite teammates to your projects as editors or viewers. Share progress, split tasks, build together even when you're apart.

🔒

Privacy Controls

Working on something stealth? Hide any project, show a vague description, or keep it completely private. You control what's visible.

📅

Calendar Sync

Events sync to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook. Never miss a session. Get reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before.
